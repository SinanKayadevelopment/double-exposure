How to Create a Double Exposure Video

  1. 1
    Upload Your Portrait: Choose a high-quality portrait photo (JPG or PNG). The AI will automatically detect and isolate the person from the background.
  2. 2
    Add Texture: Upload a video or image (ocean waves, city skyline, mountains, etc.) that will appear inside the person's silhouette.
  3. 3
    Customize: Adjust the blend intensity, add vignette fade, blur the background, and position the texture perfectly.
  4. 4
    Download: For static images, click "Download Image" to save as PNG. For videos, click "Record" to capture a 5-second clip and download as WebM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Double Exposure really free?

Yes! Double Exposure is 100% free to use. No hidden costs, no subscriptions, no watermarks. Create unlimited double exposure effects.

Do I need to create an account?

No sign-up required. Simply visit the page, upload your files, and start creating. Your privacy is our priority.

What file formats are supported?

For portraits: JPG, PNG. For textures: JPG, PNG (static images) or MP4, WebM (videos). Output: PNG for images, WebM for videos.

How do I save my creation?

For static textures, click "Download Image" to save as PNG. For video textures, click "Record (5s)" to capture a clip, then "Download Video" to save as WebM.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript and AI models. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server.

What browsers are supported?

Works best in modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure JavaScript is enabled for the AI features to work.
